THE REALITY IN BUSOGA

Across the Busoga Region (Eastern Uganda) 8 out of 10 girls are missing school because of their periods. Some have never used a sanitary pad before. Others use old cloths,paper or even leaves.They face shame, mockery, and fear simply for experiencing something natural....
