The Reality in Busoga

Across the Busoga Region (Eastern Uganda) 8 out of 10 girls are missing school because of their periods. Some have never used a sanitary pad before. Others use old cloths,paper or even leaves.They face shame, mockery, and fear simply for experiencing something natural. Every month, bright girls fall behind in class or drop out completely not because they lack intelligence but because they lack access,knowledge and dignity. SheBloom, we're here to change that,

The Reality Girls Face

  • Most girls don't understand what menstruation is or how to manage it safely.
  • Many cannot afford or access sanitary pads, leaving them to use unsafe alternatives.
  • Cultural taboos and stigma make girls feel embarassed or afraid during their periods.
  • Girls often stay home during their periods due to lack of proper care, missing social or community activties.
  • Using unhygenic materials leading to infections and long-term health problems.
  • Lack of support and guidance making girls feel alone and powerless during a natural process.

How We Help Girls Bloom
