Lend Your Heart
To Change A Girl's story.

VOLUNTEER

100% of your donation goes directly to supporting girls in need. Every month, many girls in rural communities miss school because they lack sanitary products. Your donation helps provide dignity kits, soap, and menstrual health education to girls in the Busoga region, helping them stay in school and live with confidence. Together we can make a lasting impact.

How Your Donation Helps

Each Contribution big or small creates a real impact.

Amount Impact
