SHEBLOOM DIGNITY KIT

VOLUNTEER

Fighting Period Poverty, One Kit At a Time

Our dignity kit is more than just a package of sanitary pads, it's a lifeline of confidence, care, and empowerment for girls in the Busoga region. Many girls face a silent struggle every month, missing school or hiding from daily life because they lack access to basic menstrual products. Each kit we prepare carries hope and the promise that no girl should be held back by her period. Every item is a reminder that they are valued, supported, and capable of achieving amazing things. Through these kits, we aim to break the cycle of shame, giving girls the tools, dignity, and courage to embrace each day with confidence and strength.

Why It Matters

Without access to menstrual products, many girls in our region miss school, feel embarrassed, or withdraw from daily life. Your support ensures they can attend school, participate fully in their communities, and take care of their health. Every kit delivered is a step toward equality, confidence, and brighter futures.
