Fighting Period Poverty, One Kit At a Time

Why It Matters

Without access to menstrual products, many girls in our region miss school, feel embarrassed, or withdraw from daily life. Your support ensures they can attend school, participate fully in their communities, and take care of their health. Every kit delivered is a step toward equality, confidence, and brighter futures.
