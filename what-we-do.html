How We Help Girls Bloom

SheBloom, we believe menstruation should never be a reason for a girl to hide, feel ashamed, or give up on her dreams.

Access to Sanitary Pads

We provide safe, reusabe, and affordable menstrual products through our dignity kits program so no girl has to miss out or feel ashamed during her periods.

Give a Pad Pack

Menstrual Education

We teach girls about their bodies and menstrual health, helping them understand and embrace this natural part of life with confidence.

Building Confidence

Through education and support, we help girls regain their dignity and believe in their worth and potential.

Breaking the Stigma

We create open conversations to end the shame and silence surrounding menstruation, allowing girls to feel proud and supported.

Creating Safe Places

We povide safe environments where girls can learn, share experiences, ask questions, and receive guidance freely.

Community Engagement

We involve parents, teachers, and local leaders to build communities that support girls and understand their menstrual needs.

What We're Working Toward

Expand Access to Menstrual Products

We aim to provide both disposable and reusable sanitary pads for girls who cannot afford them, so no girl has to miss school or feel ashamed during her period.

Launch a Local Production Initiative

We plan to train girls to make reusable pads,using sewing machines to make the process more efficient. This not only ensures a sustainable supply of menstrual products but also empowers girls economically.

Acquire a Mobile Outreach Van

With a van, we can reach remote schools and rural communities, bringing education, support, and hygiene products directly to the girls who need them most.

Build Safe-Space Hubs

We want to create welcoming spaces where girls can learn about menstrual health, ask questions freely, and receive ongoing support without fear or shame.

Strengthen Community Awareness

By engaging parents, teachers, and local leaders, we aim to break menstrual taboos and build a culture of understanding and respect for girls in every community.

Measure and Expand Our Impact

We are committed to documenting our work, tracking progress, and learning from every activity, so that over time, we can reach more schools, more girls, and more communities.
