Dignity Kit Distribution

Our Dignity Kits provide essential menstrual items to girls in underserved and hard-to-reach communities. For many girls, this is their first time seeing a menstrual pad.

Menstrual Education Sessions

Most girls we serve have never learned how to use a pad or what a period is. Our education sessions teach them how to manage their cycle with confidence.

Community Outreach

We engage mothers, boys, leaders, and teachers to break the stigma around menstruation.
