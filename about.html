Who We Are

When She Blooms, We All Bloom.

Proverbs 31:8-9

    In the Busoga Region of Uganda, many girls still face fear, shame, and silence when they begin menstruation. Some have never seen a sanitary pad before. Others use old cloths, rags or paper because they cannot afford proper menstrual products. Many stay home from school for days every month, falling behind or dropping out completely.

    At SheBloom we believe every girl deserves better.

    We are a faith-based community initiative working across the Busoga region to educate, support, and empower girls to manage their periods with dignity, knowledge, and confidence.

    Through school workshops, community outreach, and pad distribution, we are:

    • Breaking the silence around menstruation.
    • Teaching truth about menstrual health.
    • Providing access to safe hygiene materials
    • Restoring dignity to girls who have been mocked or shamed

    -- Because when a girl understands her body,she gains confidence.

    -- When she can attend school every day, she keeps her dreams alive.

    And when she blooms, her whole community blooms too

    Our Mission

    To ensure every girl across the Busoga region can face menstruation with dignity, stay in school, and reach her full potential through mestrual education, access to sanitary pads and community awereness.

    Our Vision

    A Busoga region where no girl is held back by her period where she is free to learn, dream, and thrive with dignity.
